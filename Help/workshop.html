Artwork

The artwork is the important part of the workshop, all your strokes will be displayed on it, it's the surface of the painting. It also can be zoomed or moved freely to fit the screen of your iPad.

You need to select your Painting Tools(such as brush or knife), and pickup color from the Palette,  and then drawing on the surface.

Here is the highlight of the available key features:

  1. Support most of Oil Painting Tools, such as Brushes and Knifes.
  2. Support color picker and pattern picker.
  3. Support undo and redo.
  4. Support Wetter and Dryer mode.
  5. Support Image import and export.
  6. Support multiple layer.
  7. Support Grids, Guide Lines, Tracing and Reference.

For more details, please read the documention of Toolbar.
