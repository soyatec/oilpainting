Some of the features of drawing oil painting are called advanced features, you need to unlock them from the store on Gallery.

Here is the list of advanced features:

  • Dryer mode

    Dry drawing for oil painting.

  • Wetter mode

    Wet drawing for oil painting.

  • Light Editor

    Nature lights control for oil painting.

  • Layer Management

    Multiple layers support for oil painting.

  • Grids, Guide Lines, Tracing and Reference

    Very useful assistance tools.
