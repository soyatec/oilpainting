Tool Controller is used to configure the Azimuth and Altitude of Painting Tools

It's deeply worked with the Apple Pencil to control the Painting Tools while drawing.

Controller

Drag on the metallic ball of it, you will find the snapshot of the painting tools(Brushes and Knifes) will be rotated.

Of course, the stroke lines of painting tools(Brushes and Knifes) will be different if this value is different.
